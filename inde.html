About ICAN Automation

Incepted in the year 2009 in Greater Noida, UP, Ican Automation Solutions is a noteworthy Manufacturer and Supplier of a wide assortment of Distribution Panels, PLC Control Panels, Automatic MCC & AMF Panels, and DG Synchronization Panels.

Manufactured using state-of-the-art machinery and optimum grade components, our products are widely demanded across industries for reliable power supplying, electricity control, and automation.

We also offer specialized Electrical Transformer Installation Services to our clients.
